About HVG

Discover the story, mission, and people behind your favorite home solutions.

Crafting Beautiful Homes Since 2010

HVG was founded on the simple belief that everyone deserves to live in a home they love. We began as a small, local tile shop in Gorakhpur with a passion for quality and an eye for cutting-edge design. Over the past decade, we have grown into a leading provider of comprehensive home solutions, but our core values remain the same.

Our journey has been one of continuous learning and curation. We travel extensively to source products from the best manufacturers, ensuring every item in our collection meets our high standards of durability, style, and craftsmanship. We don't just sell products; we provide inspiration and expertise to help you create spaces that are both beautiful and functional.

  • Customer-Centric Approach
  • Unwavering Commitment to Quality
  • A Deep Passion for Design Innovation

Our Mission

To empower every homeowner and designer by providing an unparalleled selection of high-quality, stylish, and affordable home improvement products. We strive to make the process of creating a dream home an accessible, enjoyable, and inspiring experience for everyone.

Our Vision

To be the most trusted and sought-after home solutions brand in the region, recognized for our curated collections, exceptional customer service, and our commitment to bringing the latest global design trends to our local community.
